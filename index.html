GST Classes

What Is the Goods and Services Tax (GST)? The goods and services tax (GST) may be a VAT levied on most goods and services sold for domestic consumption. The GST is paid by consumers, but it's remitted to the govt by the companies selling the products and services. In effect, GST provides revenue for the government.
The course gives a comprehensive insight into the principles of GST as well as other nuances of the indirect tax regime. It enables the candidate to gain an understanding and apprise them about the current developments concerning GST.

Tally

Tally is popularly known as an accounting software for small and medium businesses. It does all the functions of accounting that a particularly mid sized business has. This is the view of most of the persons who know about Tally. But Tally does much more than that. Tally is not just an accounting software. After the release of ERP version of Tally, the capability and functioning of Tally is much more widened and extended to a great extent.
For example (I give this example often to various people) did you know that you maintain Employees information in Tally?
